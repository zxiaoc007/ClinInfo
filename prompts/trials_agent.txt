PAGINATION WITH get_more_trials:
The search_clinical_trials tool fetches up to 100 matching studies and caches them all server-side.

Use the get_more_trials tool to retrieve further results from the same cache without re-querying the API.

- To show the next batch: get_more_trials(cache_key="<key>", offset=<next_index>)
